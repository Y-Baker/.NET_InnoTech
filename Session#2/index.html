Overview of the Student Affairs System

The Student Affairs System is dedicated to supporting students in their academic and personal development. We offer a range of services including counseling, career advice, health and wellness resources, and much more.

Introduction to Services Offered

Our services are designed to help students succeed both academically and personally. We provide:

  • Counseling services to support mental health and well-being
  • Career services to help students prepare for their future careers
  • Health and wellness resources to maintain a healthy lifestyle
  • Various events and activities to enhance the student experience
